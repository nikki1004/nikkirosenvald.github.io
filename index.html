How I think

Four Principles That
Shape Everything I Build

These aren't frameworks I found in a textbook — they're what I've learned building programs that actually had to work, for security teams, executives, and at-risk youth alike.

01
Strategy Before Content

I start every engagement by asking what behavior needs to change and why it isn't changing already. That answer shapes the curriculum — not the other way around. Action mapping, stakeholder alignment, and risk analysis happen before a single slide is made.

02
Measurement That Means Something

Completion rates tell you almost nothing. I build pre/post assessments, proficiency validations, and downstream behavior metrics into programs from day one — so when I show a 29% lift or 96% completion, there's methodology behind it.

03
Scale Without Losing the Human

I've designed for audiences of 8 and audiences of 86,000. The discipline is the same: understand who the learner actually is, what they're doing when they're not in training, and what would make this feel relevant to them specifically — even inside a global rollout.

04
Systems Thinking, Not One-Off Projects

I build reusable infrastructure — content libraries, governance models, release rhythms, wiki architectures — so organizations don't start from scratch every cycle. Sustainable programs outlast their creators.

How I work

My Design Process

Every engagement is different, but the discipline is consistent. I don't start building until I understand what the business actually needs — and why current behavior isn't matching it.

Step 01
🎯
Diagnose Before Designing

Stakeholder interviews, performance data review, audit findings analysis. I find the gap between current and desired behavior — and validate that training is actually the right solution before proposing one.

Step 02
🗂
Architecture & Alignment

Competency mapping, learning objective alignment, pathway sequencing, and framework mapping (NIST, NICE, SANS, org-specific). I build the skeleton before any content is written — and get stakeholder sign-off at this stage.

Step 03
⚙️
Build, Measure, Sustain

Develop with reuse in mind: templates, governance docs, LMS structure, content libraries. Embed measurement from the start. Set release rhythms that don't require heroic effort to maintain year over year.
